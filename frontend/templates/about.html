About BloodBridge

Saving lives by connecting hearts and veins.

Our Mission

BloodBridge was founded with a single goal: to ensure that no life is lost due to a lack of blood. In critical moments, every second counts. We provide a seamless digital platform where hospitals, blood banks, and donors can coordinate in real-time.

How It Works

1

Register

Sign up as a donor or a registered Blood Bank with verified credentials.

2

Request/Notify

Blood banks post urgent requirements; donors receive instant notifications.

3

Save Lives

Donors visit the bank, donate blood, and the inventory is updated instantly.
